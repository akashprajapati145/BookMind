The Core Premise

Atomic Habits is a definitive guide to breaking bad behaviors and adopting good ones in four steps, showing you how small, incremental, everyday routines compound into massive, positive change over time. James Clear argues that the most effective way to change your life is not by focusing on big goals, but by focusing on the systems that lead to those goals.

trending_up

1% Rule

Focus on getting 1% better every day. If you get 1% better each day for one year, you'll end up thirty-seven times better by the time you're done.

psychology

Identity Shift

True behavior change is identity change. You might start a habit because of motivation, but you’ll only stick with it if it becomes part of your identity.
